ALBA SPACE

A space initiative that makes space, science and technology accessible to everyone.

Founded in Turkey in 2022 ALBA SPACEis a leading space technology organization that takes brands into space for the first and only time in Turkey and conducts scientific experiments in the stratosphere. In the field of space technologies with its professional and expert team; It plays an important role in designing the future positively by caring about the health of the next generation and our planet, in line with the carbon neutral principle, with its social responsibility and awareness projects, training programs and innovative experience designs.

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, run the site efficiently and carry out marketing activities. In accordance with KVKK, transparency and explicit consent of users are essential for the processing of personal data through cookies.

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie banner informing you about the use of cookies. Through this banner, your explicit consent is requested for the use of non-essential cookies.

  • Explicit Consent: Your consent is obtained by an active action, such as clicking a button such as "Accept All." Continuing to use the site does not mean that you consent to the use of cookies.
  • Detailed Selection: You will be given the options to "Reject All" and "Manage Cookies". This way, you have the right to choose which categories of cookies you allow. The checkboxes for functional, analytical and marketing cookies are not pre-ticked.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can easily withdraw your consent or change your preferences at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Mandatory Cookies: It is necessary for the secure operation of the site and the fulfillment of its basic functions (e.g. shopping cart, login). Your explicit consent is not required for these cookies.
  • Functional Cookies: It gives you a more personalized experience by remembering your preferences, such as language or region.
  • Performance/Analytical Cookies: It helps us improve our service quality by collecting statistical information about how you use the site.
  • Advertising/Marketing Cookies: It is used to create a profile based on your interests and to show you relevant advertisements.
